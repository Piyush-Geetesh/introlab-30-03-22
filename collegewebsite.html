NITTE MEENAKSHI INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

NMIT admission 2021 are open for UG and PG courses. Candidates can visit the official
website to avail the online application forms. NITTE Meenakshi Institute of Technology (NMIT) is
located in Bengaluru and was established in 2001. The educational institution has received autonomous
status by UGC. It is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi, accredited by
NAAC with A Grade, its programmes are accredited by NBA and approved by AICTE. The Institute has 120+
courses, 36+ institutes and around 20,543+ current students. It offers several courses such as BE, MBA,
MTech, MCA, MSc, PhD, Diploma and Automotive Master Trainer courses. Nitte Meenakshi Institute of
Technology (NMIT) and Nitte School of Management (NSM) is collaborated with North Dakota State
University (NDSU), Fargo, USA to create two new centres of Excellence namely Centre for Systems
Innovations and Centre for Global Studies in NMIT/NSM campus.
